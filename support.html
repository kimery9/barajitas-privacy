Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab, enter your friend's unique friend code in the search box, and tap Add. They'll receive a request they can accept from their Requests tab.

How does trading work?

Once you're connected with friends, go to Friends → Trades → Browse to see trade opportunities. You can select stickers to give and receive, then send a trade request. Your friend can accept or decline it from their Incoming tab.

How do I scan a sticker?

Tap the Scan tab and point your camera at the back of the sticker (not the front). The app reads the sticker number printed on the back and marks it in your collection.

I restored my purchase but Premium isn't showing

Go to Profile → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If the issue persists, contact us below.
